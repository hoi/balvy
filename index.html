Welcome to Addies Place, "Assisted Living Like Home!"

Welcome to Addie's Place, a place for seniors that desire assisted living.
"Let us make our house yours!"
Located in rural Dewitt Michigan, Addie's Place offers its residents progressive care in a peaceful country setting.
Addie's Place is a licensed six bed Adult Foster Care Family Home for ambulatory senior adults with special needs, including particular dietary requirements, who choose or need assistance with everyday activities.
Mission
Our Mission at Addie's Place is to promote the health and independence of senior adults with special needs.

We accomplish our Mission by providing:

  • Healthy meal plans which can accommodate special dietary requirements, developed with input from a Registered Dietician.
  • 24 hour supervision and care by staff trained in the special care needs of individuals with Diabetes, Congestive Heart Failure, Stroke, Alzheimer's and related conditions.
  • A safe, comfortable environment
  • Individualized recreational activities and fitness programs
  • Housekeeping, Laundry and Transportation services.
  • Personal e-mail accounts, access to digital photography and assistance with correspondence to family and friends if needed or desired.
About the Director
Theresa Alvarado is a Registered Nurse who has worked with the Elderly for over 25 years. She obtained a Bachelor’s degree of Science in Nursing from Mercy College of Detroit and a Master’s degree in Public Administration from Western Michigan University. Her experience includes Critical, Acute, Extended and Home care settings as staff, manager, administrator and educator.
